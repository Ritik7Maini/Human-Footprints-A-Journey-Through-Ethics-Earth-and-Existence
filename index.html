Why do we treat Earth as property?

Human societies have long claimed ownership of land and resources, shaping laws and economies. But Earth itself is a shared habitat and life-support system, raising deep ethical questions about property and stewardship.

Aspect Viewpoint
Legal Land ownership grants rights to use, sell, or lease land within state laws.
Ethical Land stewardship focuses on responsibility towards environment and future generations.
Cultural Some indigenous cultures see land as communal and sacred rather than owned.

Land Ownership and Legal Rights

In India and many other countries, owning land does not equate to owning all its mineral wealth. Governments maintain rights over minerals and natural resources due to their strategic and societal importance.
